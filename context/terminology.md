# AI Terminology Glossary
- **LLM**: Large Language Model.
- **RAG**: Retrieval-Augmented Generation.
- **Evals**: Standardized tests or metrics to evaluate the performance, safety, and accuracy of AI models.
- **Token**: The basic unit of data processed by an LLM (words or sub-words).
- **Context Window**: The maximum amount of text/tokens an AI model can process at once.
- **Hallucination**: When an AI generates incorrect but plausible-sounding information.
- **Prompt Engineering**: The process of structuring text that can be interpreted and understood by a generative AI model.
- **Fine-tuning**: The process of retraining an existing AI model on a new dataset to specialize it for a specific task.
