# CardWise 💳

**CardWise** is a premium, AI-powered credit card management platform designed to maximize your rewards and bring clarity to your wallet. 

## 📖 The Story

CardWise was born out of a personal frustration shared by **Tushar and Jatin**. 

Like many people, they had accumulated a variety of credit cards, each with its own complex web of categories, rotating bonuses, and varying cashback rates. While standing in checkout lines or shopping online, they found themselves constantly second-guessing which card to pull out. Was this restaurant 3x points or 4% cashback? Does this card have a travel bonus this quarter? 

They realized that they were leaving hundreds of dollars on the table every year simply because it was impossible to keep track of every reward structure in their heads. 

Tushar and Jatin wanted a solution that wasn't just another spreadsheet or a cluttered financial app. They wanted an intelligent "digital wallet" that understood their specific cards and could provide instant, AI-driven recommendations. They built CardWise to solve this problem once and for all—creating a tool that tells you exactly which card to use for every purchase, ensuring you never miss a reward again.

## ✨ Features

- **Smart Wallet Sync**: Securely persist your personal credit card collection, ensuring your specific data is always available when you need it.
- **AI Recommendation Engine**: Simply describe where you are shopping, and CardWise analyzes your wallet against the current market to identify your optimal card.
- **Strategic Insights**: A unified dashboard providing a clear view of your annual fees and total welcome bonus potential.
- **Seamless Onboarding**: A high-speed search interface that lets you build your digital wallet in seconds.
- **Refined Aesthetics**: A premium interface built with a curated high-contrast design and clean Helvetica typography.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **Framework**: Next.js (App Router)
- **Database & Auth**: Supabase
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS
- **Icons**: Lucide React
- **Typography**: Helvetica System Stack

## 📁 Project Structure

- `src/app`: Application routing and page layouts.
- `src/components`: Feature-specific UI components (Dashboard, Wallet, Search).
- `src/lib`: Core logic, server actions, and card data utilities.
- `src/utils`: Infrastructure configurations for Supabase and middleware.

## 🚦 Getting Started

To run CardWise, ensure you have your Supabase environment variables configured (`NEXT_PUBLIC_SUPABASE_URL` and `NEXT_PUBLIC_SUPABASE_PUBLISHABLE_KEY`). Install dependencies using your preferred package manager and start the development server to begin optimizing your rewards.
